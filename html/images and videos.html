Images and Videos


Images

Images are used almost everywhere in every website it is used to look at an image,
the image tag is also a self closing tag just like input 

<img src="url or file">

Videos

Videos are used to show a video, it has many attributes like autoplay which automaticly plays the video
whwen the website is loaded and loop which repeats the video once it is finished and controls help you to do things
like fullscreen and pause 


        <video controls autoplay loop src="url or file"></video>
